Our Services

Distinguished Medical Communications offers a multitude of unique and useful services benefitting patients, practitioners, and health service providers. Simply add your desired items to your cart and we will be in contact with you as soon as possible after you checkout.

Symptoms Test

Our specialized test allows patients to communicate problems before an appointment.

Symptoms Test Demo

Quick and easy appointments between doctor and patient are possible with our symptoms test. By utilizing our test, the doctor can review possible diagnoses before meeting with the patient. This creates a faster and more efficient visit, saving both the patient and doctor valuable time.

Telemedicine

Telemedicine allows patients to reach out anytime and anywhere in the world to communicate with doctors and receive medical care. We can provide the servers and equipment needed to implement this service into your existing system.
